ABOUT

A blood donation occurs when a person voluntarily has blood drawn and used for transfusions
and/or made into biopharmaceutical medications by a process called fractionation (separation of whole blood components).
Most blood donors are unpaid volunteers who donate blood for a community supply.

Our Facilities

You can donate blood and for that, you need to be 18-65 years old, weigh 45kg or more and be fit and healthy.

It takes about 15-20 minutes to donate blood and you can donate it after every 12 weeks.

Above chart give an idea about how you can help needy of other blood groups also.
AB negative is the rarest of the eight main blood types - just 1% of our donors have it.

We keep stock of blood ready for urgent need and provide it as soon as possible.
It has always been our goal to have enough of the amount even of the rarest blood group.
